# Equipment & Hardware Purchases

Hardware and equipment purchases over $2,000 (laptops, servers, networking
gear, and similar capital-adjacent equipment) require IT department sign-off
before payment, to confirm the purchase matches an approved specification and
isn't duplicating existing inventory.

Acceptable evidence: an IT ticket or asset-request number, or an explicit
statement that IT already reviewed and approved the purchase. Purchases at or
under $2,000 do not require IT sign-off.
