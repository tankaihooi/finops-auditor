# Professional Services & Consulting Engagements

Consulting and professional services engagements over $10,000 require a
signed Statement of Work (SOW) on file and Finance Director sign-off before
payment.

Acceptable evidence: a referenced SOW or contract number, or an explicit
statement that Finance has already approved the engagement. Invoices under
$10,000 for professional services do not require SOW/Finance Director
approval, though normal vendor-approval rules still apply.
