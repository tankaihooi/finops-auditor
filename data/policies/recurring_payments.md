# Recurring Subscriptions & Retainers

Recurring subscriptions, retainers, and other regularly scheduled payments do
not require new approval each billing cycle as long as the amount and cadence
are consistent with the previously approved engagement. Only a material
increase in amount, a change in vendor or scope, or a break in the expected
cadence requires re-approval under the relevant category policy (software
subscription, professional services, etc.).

This exception exists specifically so that legitimate recurring payments are
not repeatedly re-flagged for approval every cycle.
