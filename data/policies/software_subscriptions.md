# Software & SaaS Subscription Approval

Software subscription and SaaS invoices over $500 require VP approval prior to
payment. This includes new subscriptions, renewals, and tier upgrades.

Acceptable evidence of approval: an approval ticket number, a named approver
and date, or an explicit statement that the renewal was pre-approved as part
of an annual budget cycle. If the invoice or its description does not show
any such evidence, treat it as unapproved and flag it for VP sign-off before
payment.

Subscriptions at or under $500 do not require VP approval regardless of
vendor.
